Research & Insights

How our data annotation work contributed to global conservation technology

How KAZA Data Supported the New Scoutbot Model

The Kavango–Zambezi Transfrontier Conservation Area (KAZA) is home to some of Africa's most important wildlife populations. In 2024, our team meticulously prepared a high-quality dataset of animal images and observations from the region through careful labeling, metadata validation, and accuracy checks.

Wild Me integrated this well-structured dataset into their new Scoutbot AI model, making it more accurate and faster at identifying wildlife across different habitats. What began as a regional project evolved into a global contribution—strengthening conservation technology used worldwide to protect wildlife.

Our Mission

WildData Annotation Lab is dedicated to advancing wildlife research through accurate data annotation and analytics. Our mission is to help scientists, conservationists, and AI developers unlock the potential of wildlife datasets for research, protection, and innovation.

We believe that high-quality data is the foundation of meaningful research and effective conservation efforts. By providing precise annotation services, we enable researchers to focus on analysis and insights rather than data preparation.

Our team combines expertise in data science, ecology, and machine learning to deliver services that meet the unique challenges of wildlife research.

Our Technology Stack

We use industry-leading annotation tools to ensure precision, efficiency, and quality in every wildlife research project. Our team is expertly trained on multiple platforms to meet your specific needs.

Video & Image Annotation

CVAT (Computer Vision Annotation Tool)

CVAT is a powerful open-source annotation platform designed for computer vision tasks. We use CVAT for complex video and image annotation projects that require precision and scalability.

  • Advanced video frame annotation
  • Multiple annotation formats (bounding boxes, polygons)
  • Track wildlife across video sequences
  • Collaborative team annotation workspace
Visit CVAT Website
Geospatial Annotation

SurveyScope / DetWab

SurveyScope is a specialized platform for annotating aerial and satellite imagery with geospatial context. Perfect for large-scale wildlife surveys and habitat mapping projects.

  • Location-based wildlife annotation
  • Aerial and drone imagery support
  • GPS coordinate integration
  • Large-scale survey management
Visit SurveyScope
Camera Trap Management

Scout

ScoutBeast is our go-to platform for managing and annotating camera trap imagery. It streamlines the workflow from image collection to species identification and data export.

  • Automated image organization
  • Species identification and tagging
  • Behavior classification tools
  • Time-series analysis support
