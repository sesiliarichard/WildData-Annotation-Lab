Research & Insights

How our data annotation work contributed to global conservation technology

How KAZA Data Supported the New Scoutbot Model

The Kavango–Zambezi Transfrontier Conservation Area (KAZA) is home to some of Africa's most important wildlife populations. In 2024, our team meticulously prepared a high-quality dataset of animal images and observations from the region.

Wild Me integrated this well-structured dataset into their new Scoutbot AI model, making it more accurate and faster at identifying wildlife across different habitats.

Video & Image Annotation

CVAT (Computer Vision Annotation Tool)

CVAT is a powerful open-source annotation platform designed for computer vision tasks.

  • Advanced video frame annotation
  • Multiple annotation formats
  • Track wildlife across video sequences
  • Collaborative team workspace

Geospatial Annotation

SurveyScope / DetWab

Specialized platform for annotating aerial and satellite imagery with geospatial context.

  • Location-based wildlife annotation
  • Aerial and drone imagery support
  • GPS coordinate integration
  • Large-scale survey management
Visit SurveyScope
Camera Trap Management
